Houston Wedding Photographer

Historical buildings surrounding Market Square Park, which shows off some of the rich culture that Houston, Texas has to offer. With grand city backdrops and unique cultural buildings, HTX offers some of the best venues for wedding ceremonies and receptions. So, if you are planning a wedding in Houston and are in need of a lively, talented and passionate photographer, The Light Photography is the perfect choice for you!

The Light has a very simple and relaxed style when working as a wedding photographer in Houston. We love to capture sweet and intimate moments between a couple, and share their amazing love story on film. The Light loves producing dreamy and romantic photos. We are known for capturing beautiful wedding images right before sunset, during the golden hour, where we love to incorporate sun flare that give our pictures a warm and angelic glow from the sun hitting the glass of the lens. What keeps The Light inspired as an Houston wedding photographer is producing images that represent you and your newlywed life. We enjoy thoughtfully decorated wedding spaces, soft lighting and lots of flowers! Capturing the unique beauty and special details of every Houston area wedding is what The Light does best and what we love to do. As a wedding photographer and Houston native, we love to spend spare time exploring Houston in search of new and creative spots to capture beautiful wedding pictures. Some of The Light's favorite wedding locations in Houston are the Magnolia Hotel, the Astorian, and the Park Plaza Hotel. Although taking timeless portraits of a bride and groom are our specialty, we love to capture the details of weddings including special decor, rings, the reception venue and the bridal party attire.
